01 — Identity

The mark.

A box jellyfish mascot rising from the reef — coastal, agile, a little bit dangerous. Always hold on a dark navy or clean white field; never on a busy photograph without a scrim.

Clearspace · ½× tentacle width
✓
Do — give it air.

Maintain clearspace equal to the width of one tentacle on every side.

✓
Do — pair with navy.

The mark sings against #0A1628. White is the secondary surface.

✕
Don't — recolour.

The cyan/orange ratio is fixed. No mono red, no green.

✕
Don't — squish or rotate.

The wordmark and creature are one lockup. Scale together, never independently.

✕
Don't — drop on photos.

Use a 70%+ navy scrim if a photographic background is unavoidable.

06 — Voice

Tropical, tough, mates.

Aussie sport vernacular. Direct, warm, never corporate. Short sentences, a little dry humour, no jargon. We get amongst it; we don't leverage opportunities.

Tone

Confident and welcoming. Like a teammate slapping you on the back at half-time.

"Lace up the boots and get amongst it. New players always welcome."

Cadence

Short. Punchy. Then a longer line for context. Read everything aloud — if you stumble, cut it.

"More than a team. Built on mateship, competition, and a love of the game."

Vocabulary

Footy terms over corporate ones. Use FNQ, jersey, rego, fixture, ruck. Avoid solutions, journey, community-driven.

"Ready to pull on the Stingers jersey? Rego is $90 via PlayHQ."
